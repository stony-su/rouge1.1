Jester Hero — design concepts

Four candidate redesigns for the Jester (currently the curse/harlequin hero). Each canvas is a live mock of how the ball, its spawned projectiles, and its attack would animate in-engine — same dark palette and circle/line drawing style as Ball Pit X. All four lean into the jester/fool/harlequin theme but play very differently.

Tell me which one to build (or mix elements) and I'll implement it in Lua for both builds.
The little squares up top are stand-in “bricks” (enemies); watch each ball idle, then attack.
Card Sharp

Card Sharp

A harlequin gambler that flings spinning playing cards — each suit rolls a different effect, so its damage is pure showman's luck. Closest to today's jester, but built around chance.

Attack mechanic — “Wild Hand”

  • Flings spinning cards at nearby bricks, fast and often (a fan of 1–2 per throw).
  • Each card's suit rolls an effect: ♦ bonus damage, ♠ a vulnerability mark, ♣ a small splash, ♥ heals a sliver of player HP.
  • Land matching suits in a row → a “Royal Flush” burst (big multi-hit).

The projectile — Card

  • A spinning playing card with a suit pip; homes slightly onto a brick, then bursts in its suit colour.

Animation & feel

Shape
diamond-checker harlequin orb under a two-horned bell cap
Idle
bob, slow checker spin, cap bells sway
Attack
a flourish flick + a fan of cards sprays out
Trail
confetti + the odd drifting card
Aura
two-tone motley shimmer ring
Movement
fast & bouncy
Trajectory
a restless weave with flicks on each bounce
Juggler

Juggler

A poised performer keeping a cascade of props in the air and hurling one at a time — an endless round-robin of juggled throws. Steady, rhythmic, hypnotic.

Attack mechanic — “Three-Ball Cascade”

  • Keeps 3 props orbiting; each beat it hurls the next one at the nearest brick and catches a fresh one.
  • An endless round-robin cascade — a steady stream of hits, never a big windup.
  • Higher level juggles more props (4–5) → a faster effective fire rate.

The projectile — Prop

  • A juggling ball or club, flung spinning at a brick; bonks and bursts. (Could ricochet between two.)

Animation & feel

Shape
a round performer orb with three coloured props orbiting it
Idle
props circle smoothly, body bobs in time
Attack
one prop peels off the orbit and flies; a new one fades in
Trail
a faint blur of the orbit path
Aura
a soft ring marking the orbit
Movement
medium, balanced, poised (gentle)
Trajectory
smooth with little hops — never erratic
Jack-in-the-Box

Jack-in-the-Box

A wind-up prank box that hoards energy, then SPRINGS a surprise — coil-puppets boinging out everywhere. A charge-and-release rhythm: dangerous the longer it waits.

Attack mechanic — “Wind-Up Surprise”

  • Winds up over a few seconds (jittering harder the closer it gets — a clear telegraph).
  • Then SPRINGS: a fan of coil-puppets boing out, bonk bricks, and snap back, plus a confetti shockwave.
  • The longer the wind-up, the bigger the pop (held charge = more springs / damage).

The projectile — Spring-Pop

  • A coiled spring that extends from the box to a brick (puppet head on the end), strikes, and retracts.

Animation & feel

Shape
a checkered wind-up box with a turning crank + popping lid
Idle

Attack
lid bursts open, coil-puppets spring out + a shockwave
Trail
wind-up sparkles
Aura
a jittery “about to pop” shimmer that ramps up
Movement
dampened wind-up, then a springy burst (stop-start)
Trajectory
coils that boing out and retract on a tether
Puppeteer

Puppeteer

A marionette master that lowers dancing puppets on strings to do its dirty work — a control build that makes bricks “dance” (slow + vulnerable) before cutting them loose.

Attack mechanic — “Pull the Strings”

  • Lowers marionette minions on strings that dance over to a brick and slash it.
  • Struck bricks are “made to dance” — slowed and take extra damage for a beat.
  • Lv3 cuts the strings → the loose puppets fly free as homing knives.

The projectile — Marionette

  • A little puppet lowered on a visible string, dancing to its target; the string stays tethered to the ball.

Animation & feel

Shape
a fool's control-cross with a marionette dangling on strings below
Idle
the puppet dangles + sways; the cross tilts with motion
Attack
a puppet drops down a string toward a brick and slashes
Trail
trailing string threads + a dangling puppet
Aura
faint string-cross shimmer
Movement
marionette jerk — stop-start, string-pulled
Trajectory
jerky lerps + dangling sway, with string tethers
