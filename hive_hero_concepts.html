Hive Hero — design concepts

Four candidate designs for the Hive hero (a rework of the infestor, kept under the same key so the draft pool & the Hive paddle keep working). Each canvas is a live mock of how the ball, its bug projectiles, and its attack would animate in-engine — same dark palette and circle/line drawing style as Ball Pit X.

Tell me which one to build (or mix elements across them) and I'll implement it in Lua for both builds.
The little squares up top are stand-in “bricks” (enemies); watch each ball idle, then attack and send its swarm at them.
Hornet's Nest

Hornet's Nest

An angry papery wasp-nest that vents a steady stream of homing hornets — the most iconic “hive”, and it fits the infestor's orange. Recommended.

Attack mechanic — “Swarm Vent”

  • Continuously vents hornets at any brick in range (a steady stream, not a big timer).
  • Each ball bounce agitates the nest → an instant burst of ~5 hornets.
  • Charge / hero level widen the stream (bigger swarm, faster vent).

The bug — Hornet

  • Fast homing wasp; curves onto the nearest brick, stings (damage + a brief poison DoT), then dies. Beating wings + stinger.

Animation & feel

Shape
layered paper-nest orb, hex “mouth” at the base, glowing amber core
Idle
nest breathes, core pulses, 2–3 wasps orbit it
Attack
mouth flares open, a puff of wasps erupts toward the target
Trail
drifting paper flecks + one escort wasp
Aura
angry buzz heat-shimmer ring
Movement
medium speed, bouncy
Trajectory
nervous jittery weave (a buzzing path)
Honeycomb Brood

Honeycomb Brood

A golden queen's hive that opens its cells in timed waves and leaves slowing honey on the field — heavier and more zone-control than the others.

Attack mechanic — “Brood Cycle”

  • Every few seconds, hex cells flip open and launch a coordinated wave of bees.
  • A bee-kill drops a honey splat — a lingering puddle that slows bricks in it.
  • Lv3 frenzy: every bee-kill births another bee (chain reproduction).

The bug — Worker Bee

  • Chunky striped bee; headbutts a brick (damage), can ferry to a 2nd brick, and leaves honey where it kills.

Animation & feel

Shape
golden hexagon honeycomb cluster, dripping honey, a regal “queen” core
Idle
cells shimmer, honey drips, a worker bee crawls the rim
Attack
outer cells flip open white→gold and bees pour out
Trail
honey droplets (fall on gravity) + golden pollen
Aura
warm golden glow
Movement
slow, heavy, dampened
Trajectory
lazy, weighty, honey-laden arc droop
Locust Cloud

Locust Cloud

Not a solid ball at all — a roiling living swarm that drizzles darting locusts relentlessly. The most visually unique & chaotic option.

Attack mechanic — “Swarm Pressure”

  • A relentless drizzle of locusts at bricks in a wide radius (high rate, tiny hits).
  • Denser brick clusters draw more locusts — pressure scales with the crowd.
  • A kill triggers a brief feeding frenzy (fire-rate spike).

The bug — Locust

  • Erratic darting bug fired in scattering volleys; zigzags to a brick, gnaws, and a few ricochet onward to a 2nd.

Animation & feel

Shape
a churning cloud of tiny locusts loosely held in a ball, dark dense core
Idle
the whole body roils; bugs crawl over the surface; silhouette pulses
Attack
the cloud bulges toward the target and ejects a spray
Trail
a smear of stray locusts peeling off behind
Aura
hazy dark cloud + flickering wing-glints
Movement
fast, twitchy, erratic
Trajectory
loose, scattering, unpredictable drift
Spider Brood

Spider Brood

A silk egg-sac that flings web-tethering spiderlings — a control build that sticks the swarm in place with slowing web. (“Bugs” in the colloquial sense.)

Attack mechanic — “Web & Brood”

  • Fires spiderlings that shoot a web-line to a brick, bite, and stick it (web-slow).
  • A kill leaves a sticky web patch — a zone that slows bricks crossing it.
  • Lv3 spins a web between two near bricks that damages anything crossing it.

The bug — Spiderling

  • Small spider that rides a silk line to its target, bites (damage + slow), and leaves web.

Animation & feel

Shape
silk egg-sac wrapped in webbing, bulbous spider core, folded legs
Idle
legs twitch, sac pulses, a spiderling rappels down a silk line & climbs back
Attack
the sac splits and spiderlings burst out on web lines
Trail
trailing silk threads + a dangling spiderling
Aura
faint shimmering web halo
Movement
medium; “hangs” (dampened pause) then darts
Trajectory
stop-start scuttle, with web-line tethers
